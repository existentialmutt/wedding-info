Information for Travelers

The closest airports to fly into are Hartford, CT or Manchester, NH. They're both about 1 1/2 hour drive away.

There are a few beds available in the farmhouse at Pullman Farm, and it's possible to camp there too. Get in touch if this is of interest.

If you prefer more private accomodations, there are hotels and motels in Brattleboro (e.g. Latchis Hotel, Colonial Motel, Holiday Inn)

There are also many nice Airbnb's in the area. These 2 houses in West Dummerston Village can each house up to six people and are a 5 minute drive from the wedding:
